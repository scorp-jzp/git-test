Naengmeyon

cold noodles

Naengmyeon is a Korean dish consisting of long, thin noodles made from the flour and starch of various ingredients, including buckwheat, potatoes, and sweet potatoes. It is typically served cold with a tangy, refreshing broth, making it a popular choice during the hot summer months. The dish is often garnished with slices of cucumber, pear, boiled egg, and sometimes beef.

  • Ingredients:
  • 1 package of naengmyeon noodles
  • 1/2 cup of beef broth
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1/2 cucumber, julienned
  • 1/2 pear, julienned
  • 1 boiled egg, sliced
  • 1/4 pound of beef, thinly sliced
  1. Instructions:
  2. Boil the naengmyeon noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Drain and rinse the noodles under cold water to cool them down.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together the beef broth, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sugar, and sesame oil to make the broth.
  5. Divide the noodles into serving bowls and pour the broth over them.
  6. Garnish with cucumber, pear, boiled egg, and beef slices.
  7. Enjoy your refreshing bowl of naengmyeon!
